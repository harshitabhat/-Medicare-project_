Medicare is your app for successful use of medications, tablets and pills
With us you can keep track of which medications you need to take and when. For older people in particular, taking medication regularly is often a major challenge. Forgetting to take medication poses a danger that should not be underestimated for the people in question and for those around you. Choose Medicare now and keep track of all relevant medications

After you have initially created a medication that is relevant to you, a separate MediCard is created for each day of use.
This MediCard contains the name, description, day of administration and the time of day at which a medication should be taken.
If it is time to take the medication, the text turns gray and your Medicard receives the badge “Take medication now”.
With the buttons on the MediCard you have the option of marking the medication as taken and thus deleting it or adjusting the intake.
